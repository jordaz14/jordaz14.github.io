  • Fluid simulation is a computational technique, widely used in computer graphics, engineering, meteorology, and physics, to model the behavior of fluids in various environments, helping to explain phenomena likes waves and turbulence. Additionally, there are two main times of fluid simulation methods: Eulerian and Lagrangian. Eulerian methods are grid-based, computing fluid properties in each grid cell, whereas Lagrangian methods are particle-based and calculate the motion of particles.

Aside from adding some finishing touches to the style of my chrome extension, I tweaked with the OpenAI API a lot. I'm not sure I'd consider it prompt engineering, but changing the tone and structure of prompts had a huge change in the quality of its responses. For example, I noticed that asking ChatGPT to identify the mood of a topic and then recommend a song related to the mood was a bit superfluous & that it produced much better responses if I simply asked it to provides songs related to the topic.
